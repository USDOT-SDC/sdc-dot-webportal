Welcome to the Secure Data Commons Platform (SDC), a sandbox environment in AWS managed by U.S. Department of transportation. The SDC platform provides a collaborative environment for traffic engineers, researchers, data scientists and anyone who is interested in carrying out research and analysis on different datasets related to traffic, weather, crashes etc.

Ready to Get Started? Follow the steps below or directly jump to any of the sections that you wish to access. SDC Users will have the following access in the platform:

  Curated and published datasets Check out the list of currently available datasets (curated, published), data dictionaries owned by you and others. Learn more about what curated and published datasets mean
 Access to workstations with programming tools Access the workstations assigned to you, if you have requested as part of the registration process
 Bring your own datasets / algorithms Learn more about how you can bring your algorithms and code to the SDC platform
 Publish your datasets / algorithms Learn more about how you can publish your research analysis and algorithms with other researchers
